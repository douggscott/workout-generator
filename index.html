What it does

Plan your split

One to seven training days, whatever day your week starts on. Push, pull, legs or fully custom. The generator orders every plan compound-first, isolation after — and lets you choose exactly how many of each, plus whether to add ab/core work.

Log every set

Your last session's numbers sit under every set as the target to beat. Tick a set, the rest countdown starts. Warm-up, working, superset, drop set and failure — all tracked.

Break records

Personal records are detected automatically — the set turns gold the moment you earn it. Estimated one-rep max recalculates live on every lift.

Load the bar right

The plate calculator shows exactly which plates go on each side, plus warm-up sets at 40, 60 and 80 percent. Full kilogram and pound support, including the plate maths.

Syncs with Apple Health

Every completed workout logs straight to Apple Health automatically — no manual entry, no separate tracking.

Two gyms? Sorted.

Save your gym's actual equipment once — every brand, every machine — and BRBL remembers it for good. Every plan it generates from then on builds itself entirely from what you've actually got. Train somewhere else too? Save that gym separately and switch between them in a tap.

See the progress

Strength graphs per exercise, weekly muscle-group balance, a training calendar, body weight trends, and your complete history — exportable to Apple Health or CSV any time.

Yours, everywhere

Works fully offline in the gym basement. Sign in and it syncs across your devices. 24 colour themes. No ads. No paywall on your own data.

Questions

Is BRBL free?

Yes. The core tracker is free — plan, log, PRs, plate calculator, history. BRBL Pro unlocks brand-specific equipment, the smart generator, and deeper tools — but your data is never behind a paywall.

Does it work offline?

Completely. Everything runs on your phone. An account is only needed if you want your data synced across devices.

My gym's brand isn't listed — what do I do?

You can create custom exercises for anything, and the brand database grows with every update. Tell us what's missing on Instagram @brbl.app.

kg or lbs?

Both — fully. The toggle changes every number in the app including the plate calculator's maths and bar weights.

Android?

iPhone first. Android is on the roadmap — join the list above and you'll hear when it lands.
